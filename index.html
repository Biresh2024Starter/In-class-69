Mayfair Heritage, Puri

i had visited on may 15.

Mayfair Heritage Puri is an exquisite hotel that stands as a tranquil haven in the vibrant city of Puri, Odisha. It combines luxury with cultural charm, offering guests a stay that’s both comfortable and rich in local flavor. Located near the revered Jagannath Temple and the beautiful Puri Beach, it’s a favorite for those seeking spiritual rejuvenation or a peaceful beach getaway. The property features tastefully decorated rooms, delightful dining options, and exceptional service, ensuring a memorable experience by the Bay of Bengal.
